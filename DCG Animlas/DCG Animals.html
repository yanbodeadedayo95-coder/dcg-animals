DOGS

Dogs have elvoled with humans, forming strong bonds.
They comunicate feelings through body language like tail wags and ear positions.
Each dog is unique, using different signals to show emotions.
Understanding these helps build a closer, happier relationship

Things Dogs love

Dogs have simple joys that makes their tails wag and hearts happy.
Here are a few of their favorite things:

  1. Chest rubs and butt scrathes - their favorite!
  2. Calm, happy vibes from you.
  3. Interactive toys that challenge their brain

Things Dogs hates



Dogs have their own little pet peeves that can make them uncomfortable or stressed. Here are some things many dogs aren't fans of:

  1. Loud noises like fireworks, vacuum cleaners, and smoke alarms.
  2. Being hugged too tightly or sudden touches on the head.
  3. Starring too long or direct eye contact.
  4. Unfamilliar or rolling objects like bikes, strollers, and suitcases
